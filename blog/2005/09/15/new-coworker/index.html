New Coworker

Sep 15th, 2005

We added a new member to the team at work:

I think he has a bright future. I figured it had more caffeine than regular Dew, but that doesn’t appear to be the case. 36mg each. Check out that MDX though, 47. I need a source for that junk. I would probably have to go to some seedy neighborhood and buy it from a guy in a broken down Impalla. I think I would do it though.

Seriously, this stuff is pretty good. I drink a decent amount of Mountain Dew, and the little twist they put on PBII meets with my approval. I wouldn’t recommend picking up a few cases without sampling it first. But that’s what we did and it turned out okay.
